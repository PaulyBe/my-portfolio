About Me

I moved to Vienna in 2020, to pursue my Master's degree in International Business Administration at the University of Vienna. During my studies I started working in Consulting. For two years I worked at PwC in Risk Management.

During my studies and my time in consulting, I developed an interest for programming. Therefore, I decided to leave consulting and study web development.

I started doing an online program by Meta, called Meta Frontend Developer, which I completed in June 2025. The program focused on the basics of web development, especially with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Furthermore, the course taught developing web applications in React.

Since I completed the course, I have been working on several projects to improve my coding skills and to build a portfolio of my work.


In my freetime I love to play and watch tennis, ride my motorcycle and I collect old vinyls.
